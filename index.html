Petrichor is the smell of rain. The word comes from the Greek words 'petra', meaning stone, and 'ichor', which in Greek mythology refers to the golden fluid that flows in the veins of the immortals.

The common raindrop isn't actually shaped like a teardrop, but more like a hamburger bun!

The name Raine is both a boy's name and a girl's name of French origin meaning queen .

"When cows are lying down in a field, rain is on its way" -Wikipedia Weather Lore-
